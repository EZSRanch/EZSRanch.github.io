A FEW WORDS ABOUT US

EZS Ranch Services is a small family business just outside of San Marcos, Texas. After a long career with Caterpillar Inc. making construction equipment and engines, Mark and his family have settled down in the Texas Hill Country and started this business to put some of this equipment to work. Our focus is helping Texas property owners to transform rough and overgrown acreage into useful pasture land, homesites, and recreation areas.

Please contact Mark at EZS.Ranch.Services@gmail.com or call him at (956)857-4025 with any questions or to have us take a look at your job.
